Direct File

You're receiving this email because you agreed to get a survey from the IRS on your experience with Direct File. Direct File is an online tax filing tool that lets you file your federal tax return directly with the IRS.

We'd like to get your feedback to help improve Direct File. The survey should take about 8 to 10 minutes to complete. Participation is voluntary. Your decision to respond to the survey won't impact you and your federal tax return. Your responses will be used by the Direct File team to improve Direct File.

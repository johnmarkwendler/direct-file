Direct File

Submit your 2024 federal tax return by April 15

If you haven't yet filed your federal tax return, you can still file with Direct File, which now also supports filing with retirement income (Form 1099-R).

The federal tax filing deadline is April 15 at 11:59 PM in your time zone. Your deadline may be postponed if you were affected by a disaster situation.

Sign in to Direct File to submit your federal tax return before the deadline.

What's next?

You'll get an email from Direct File once your federal tax return has been accepted or rejected. This usually takes just a few minutes, but can take up to an hour.

  • If your federal tax return is accepted, you'll receive instructions to pay your tax balance or check the status of your tax refund
  • If your federal tax return is rejected, you'll receive instructions to fix your tax return and resubmit or find other ways to file your taxes

Don't forget about state taxes and credits

Depending on your situation, you may have to file a state tax return in addition to a federal tax return. You may also be eligible for state tax credits.

If you need to file a state return, sign in to Direct File to be guided to your state's free tax filing tool.

Please check your state's tax filing deadline.
